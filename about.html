About Us

Our Philosophy

Style is a Way to Say Who You Are

Founded by the passionate stylist and digital creator Danish Ansari, Mr Danish Hair Salon is not just a place to get a haircut—it's a luxury grooming studio where self-expression meets meticulous craft. Located in the heart of Deoband, we have built a reputation for pioneering modern fades, classic trims, and custom hair treatments.

Beyond the salon chairs, Danish Ansari is a celebrated Instagram Reels Creator with over 27,000 followers. He showcases stunning hair transformations, sharing styling tutorials, fades secrets, and grooming hacks that inspire thousands of clients and fellow barbers across the region.

Standards of Excellence

  • Elite Artistry: Tailored haircuts crafted specifically for your hair type, scalp structure, and style choices.
  • Hygiene First: All tools are sterilized using UV sanitizers, and we maintain an absolute clean space.
  • Social Influence: Bringing viral internet hair trends straight to your local neighborhood in Deoband.
